F- distribution 
-----------------

- distribution related to chi square distribution 
- lets say you have two chi square distribution and you divide both the distributions with their respective degree of freedoms. and do the division of both you will get F distribution.

-  F - Distribution ->  (chi1/df1)/(chi2/df2)



- it is the continous probabibilty distribution (PDF)
- there are no negative values and tail is on the right side
- used in ANOVA Test(Analysis of variance)




Parameters 
-------------

1. Numerator df
2. Denomiator df 



Used for
-----------

1. Used two test equality of two variances 
2. Used in Anova
