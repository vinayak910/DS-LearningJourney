Q Why cant single table hold all the data?
ans: Because of two major reasons 
	1. data reduncy
	2. anomalies
---------------------------------------------------------------------

	1. data reduncy means repeatition of data
	2. Different anomalies. a- Insertion , b. deletion , c. Update

-------------------------------------------------------------------------

solution - Data Normalization:
	data should be stored in different tables rather than one , to reduce 
	data reduncy and dependency.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
different levels of normalization: refer pdf

1nf: 	each data should be in each different column(No comma separatedvalues 	in single column)
------------------------------------------------------------------

2nf : should be in 1nf
      should not partial dependent
      means the non key attributes should not be dependent partially but on the whole primary key.

ex - Composite primary key made up of 2 keys , if non key attributes is dependent on only one of part of composite primary key then it is not in 2nf.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

3nf: should be in 2nf
     should not be transitive dependent
     transitive dependent - when non attribute key is dependent on another     	non attribute key.
